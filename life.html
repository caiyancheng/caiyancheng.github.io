Paragliding 滑翔伞


I started paragliding in April 2023, under the guidance of the national champion Wang Hongji, and I am currently studying to obtain the B license of the China Aviation Association paragliding.
我于2023年4月份开始入门滑翔伞运动, 受到国家冠军王宏吉教练指导, 目前在学习考取中国航协滑翔伞B照。

Mountaineering 登山


I started snow mountain climbing in July 2023, and I am currently challenging the highest peak in the eastern part of Kunlun Mountains - Mount Yuzhu with an altitude of 6178 meters. I plan to successfully climb Mount Everest in about 5 years.
我于2023年7月份开始入门攀登雪山运动, 目前在挑战昆仑山东段最高峰 - 海拔6178米的玉珠峰。我计划在5年时间左右成功攀登珠峰。

Free diving 自由潜水


I started freediving in April 2023, and I have passed the AIDA one-star freediving assessment. My maximum dive depth without an oxygen tank is 12m, and I am in the process of obtaining an AIDA two-star free diver license.
我于2023年4月份开始入门自由潜水运动, 目前已经通过AIDA一星自由潜水员考核。无氧气瓶我的最大下潜深度为12m, 正在考取AIDA二星自由潜水员执照。

Skiing 滑雪


I started skiing in December 2019, and have skied in Zhaojin, Cuihuashan, and Bailuyuan, Shaanxi. At present, I can adapt to the intermediate and high-level trails, that is, the black-class ski trails.
我于2019年12月份开始入门双板滑雪运动, 在陕西照金、翠华山、白鹿原进行过滑雪。目前我能够适应中高级道, 即黑级雪道。
